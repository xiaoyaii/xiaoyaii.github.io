Building stone industry will benefit from the trend of developing green buildings

Writer： admin Time：2023-03-16 18:09:33 Browse：8℃

With the enhancement of environmental awareness and the progress of building technology, the development of green buildings has been recognized by more and more people. Green building is one of the main trends of the construction industry in the future, and building materials as the foundation are expected to benefit. Green building materials have become the focus of building materials industry development in this century, especially building stone.

Among many green building materials, stone can stand out. On the one hand, it is the earliest building material in human history, with high compressive strength, good wear resistance and durability; On the other hand, stone resources are widely distributed, rich in reserves, convenient for local materials, and are common materials for all kinds of buildings.

Because of the excellent performance of stone, people began to use building stone very early. From repairing and paving roads to building palaces and temples, stone has left a heavy mark in the history of architecture.

Building stone

At present, building stone is mainly used for stone carving, garden decoration, building decoration and other purposes. There are various types of stone materials used for the above purposes, including rubble, block stone, plastic block stone, special-shaped products, synthetic products, artificial products, etc.

When selecting building stones, the applicability of various stones should be measured according to the use needs. For example, for load-bearing components, certain compressive strength should be met; For the enclosure structure, it should have good thermal insulation and wind resistance; For decorative components, consideration should be given to polishing, pattern color, etc.

In addition, there are certain requirements for the quality of stone. According to the analysis of the Analysis Report on the Strategic Investment Planning of China's Building Stone Industry Market issued by the Prospective Industry Research Institute, the first consideration of building stone is the consistency between the appearance and design requirements, and the second is the corresponding physical and chemical performance test for important parts according to the standards.

After the industrial revolution, although steel, glass, concrete and other new building materials have increased significantly, stone has not been eliminated, but has gained new life through the technological achievements of the industrial revolution. From mining, processing to transportation, a large number of new mechanical equipment are widely used in the stone industry.

At the same time, with the progress of science and technology and the demand of human sustainable development, as well as the increasing importance of environmental protection and maintenance of ecological balance, stone has gained attention again with its excellent green performance, and is expected to obtain new development opportunities in the future.

It is worth noting that in order to realize the true greening of building stone, it is necessary to carry out all aspects of stone exploration, mining, processing, application and recycling, so as to avoid only partial or partial greening.

In general, the development of green buildings is the general trend. In the promotion of green building, stone, as one of the main materials, should give full play to the green degree of building stone. The building stone industry should also increase the research and development of new products and new applications, and integrate green technology in the development, production and application.
